Hearthstone is a great game but its competitive ladder has a fatal flaw!

Users join a queue where they are matched against other players

Players start at rank 20 and can improve until rank 1. The ladder selects another player of the same rank to play against. When you win you gain ranking points, when you lose you lose them.

This system works well

Strong players win a lot and improve in rank, weak players don't advance. Players quickly advance to a rank where others of similar skill are. Most matches are competitive because of the similar strength of players.

This works great until...

The ladder resets every month

Everyone is the same level

The best players are now playing against the worst. The average difference in skill between players sky rockets. Most games are one sided, not fun for either party.
This is excacerbated because...

The problem lasts longer than you'd think

Ye

Negative feedback loops are built into the game

Noone can leave rank 20 until better players leave. The best players are playing against other good players, slowing their ranking up. Any mediocre player who improves in rank to be pushed back.

What other options should Blizzard consider?

Don't reset the ladder so often

Players like improving rank and resetting the ranks allows that more often The first week of every month much will be terrible games. Good players are wasting their time. New and weak players are getting stomped.

Use an MMR based match making system

Other Blizzard games predict players skill based on their past results. Using this MMR tool allows for better matchings, improving the game for everyone.
